What I do

Miniature Painting

I paint all types of miniatures, and concentrate on Games Workshop, Dungeons and Dragons, and several boardgame universes. Individual models that I have completed will soon be available on ebay, and some are also available as consignment items in local game stores. Details will follow soon!

Painting Commissions

Got a project in mind but you want someone to do the painting? Drop me an email and let's discuss your needs. I offer several levels of detail on miniatures of all types. Let me know your requirements and I will quote you on the completed project. Results are guaranteed!

Kit Building

If you don't want to build your models, I can do that for you as well. Very economical prices based on mini types and quantity. If you have a specific army or kit you want to deploy, I am able to do all the work for you; buy the kit, build it, and paint it. All you have to do is take them out of the box and start to play after the commission is completed :-)

My Work

Who I am

Miniature painter and builder based out of London Ontario Canada

I have a deep background in computer science, and have recently retired from that to pursue full time mini painting, which is where my passion lies. In addition to my love of painting, I am an avid guitar player with over 40 years of playing and teaching experience.I have been model building since I was about 6 years old (and that is a long time ago).

I live in London Ontario Canada, a great place with an enthusiastic gaming community.
